Beef Sausages (Hot Dog) Details

Key Ingredients

  • **Beef** (Frozen or Ground, Max 20% fat content).
  • Edible collagen casing or natural casing.
  • **Binders:** Soy protein, modified food starch, or egg albumen.
  • **Natural Spices** (Table salt, Black Pepper, Dried Garlic, Paprika, Mustard).
  • Sodium Nitrite (**E250**) as a preservative.
  • Corn syrup or Sugar (for color and texture enhancement).

**Allergy Alert:** This product may contain **Soy**, **Gluten**, **Egg**, and **Mustard**.

Cooking and Preparation Instructions

  1. **Thawing:** Hot dogs must be fully thawed in the refrigerator (not at room temperature).
  2. **Pan-Frying:** Cook in a hot pan with a little oil for **5-8 minutes** until golden brown.
  3. **Oven Preparation:** Place in a preheated oven (177°C) for approximately **15 minutes**.
  4. **Safety Check:** For safety, the internal temperature of the sausage must reach **74°C**.
