Beef Sausages Details

Key Ingredients

  • **High-Quality Ground Beef** (Fat content not exceeding 20%).
  • Natural/Artificial thin edible casing.
  • Sausage Spices (Garlic, Paprika, Fennel, Red Pepper).
  • Iced water and salt (to aid binding and preservation).

**Allergy Alert:** This product is gluten-free but contains a small amount of added sugar (Dextrose).

Nutrition Facts (Per 100 grams)

Content Value

Cooking and Preparation Instructions

  1. **Thawing:** Sausages must be fully thawed in the refrigerator before cooking.
  2. **Grilling:** Cook over medium heat for **10-12 minutes**, turning constantly until lightly browned.
  3. **Pan-Frying:** Fry in a small amount of hot oil (not deep-fried) for **8-10 minutes**.
  4. **Verification:** Use a thermometer to ensure the internal temperature reaches **74°C** for safety.
