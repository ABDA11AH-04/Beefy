Beef Sausages Details

Key Ingredients

  • **High-Quality Ground Beef** (Max 20% fat content).
  • Natural Casing (sheep intestine or edible collagen).
  • **Natural Spices** (Table salt, Black Pepper, Dried Garlic, Paprika).
  • Natural Preservatives (such as nitrates in a safe and permissible concentration).
  • Iced water and stabilizers (to aid binding).

**Allergy Alert:** This product is gluten-free, but may contain traces of **Dextrose sugar** to improve color and texture.

Cooking and Preparation Instructions

  1. **Thawing:** Sausages must be fully thawed in the refrigerator (not at room temperature).
  2. **Grilling/Pan-Frying:** Cook over medium heat for **10 to 12 minutes**, turning constantly to ensure even cooking.
  3. **Frying:** In a hot pan with a small amount of oil, fry for **8 minutes** until golden brown.
  4. **Safety Check:** To ensure safety, the internal temperature of the sausage must reach **74°C**.
