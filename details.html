Beef Sausages (Hot Dog) Details

How to use the New Packaging

This product comes in a high-quality vacuum-sealed bag featuring a one-way valve system for optimal freshness and preservation after first use.

  1. **Open and Use:** Open the package using the **Zip Lock** mechanism only. Do not cut the bag.
  2. **Dispense:** Take the desired amount of product and reseal the Zip Lock completely.
  3. **Re-vacuum:** Locate the one-way valve opening at the top of the bag. Gently press down on the bag to expel the air through the valve, thus re-creating a near-vacuum seal.
  4. **Store:** Store the re-sealed, air-reduced package back in the refrigerator.

Cooking and Preparation Instructions

  1. Safe Thawing: Hot dogs must be fully thawed in the refrigerator before cooking.
  2. Pan Method (Recommended): Cook in a hot pan with a small amount of oil or butter for 5-8 minutes, turning constantly until evenly browned.
  3. Oven Method: Place in a preheated oven (177°C / 350°F) for approximately 15 minutes.
  4. Safety Check: To ensure doneness, the internal temperature of the sausage must reach 74°C (165°F) using a food thermometer.
